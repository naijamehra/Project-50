Interesting Facts about Chameleons:

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  2. Changing the colour of their skin is an important part of communication among Chameleons
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts about Pigeons:

  • Pigeons are known for their excellent hearing abilities
  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent birds
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities

Interesting Facts about Camels:

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes
  2. When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go
  3. Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

Interesting Facts about Pandas:

  • Pandas eyes are different from normal bears- they have vertical slits for pupils
  • Giant pandas spend 10-16 hours a day feeding, mainly on bamboo
  • Pandas need at least 2 different bamboo species in their range to avoid starvation
